Introduction

The Kasangga ng Batang Kankaloo Association Inc. proudly partners with the Emmanuel John Institute of Science and Technology (EJIST), a leading educational institution in the Philippines dedicated to providing quality, career-oriented education. This partnership ensures that our members have exclusive access to high-quality academic and technical programs, helping young learners achieve their dreams and build a strong foundation for their future.
