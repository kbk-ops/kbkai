Emmanuel John Institute of Science and Technology (EJIST) – Partner of Kasangga ng Batang Kankaloo Association Inc. (KBKai)


Introduction

Kasangga ng Batang Kankaloo Association Inc. (KBKai) proudly announce the partnership with Emmanuel John Institute of Science and Technology (EJIST), a leading educational institution in the Philippines dedicated to providing quality, career-oriented education. This partnership ensures that our members have exclusive access to high-quality academic and technical programs, helping young learners achieve their dreams and build a strong foundation for their future.


Who is EJIST

EJIST is committed to nurturing students into competent, responsible, and socially conscious individuals. With modern facilities, skilled faculty, and a holistic approach to education, EJIST offers programs that combine academic excellence with technical skills, preparing students for college, careers, and life.


Campuses

EJIST has several campuses to serve students across Luzon, ensuring accessibility for all members:

  • Caloocan, Metro Manila – 237 M. H. Del Pilar Street, Grace Park, Caloocan City
  • Valenzuela, Metro Manila – 254 Marvic Building, McArthur Highway, Karuhatan, Valenzuela City
  • Pililla, Rizal – Sitio Bulacan 1, Barangay Bagumbayan, Pililla, Rizal
  • Meycauayan, Bulacan – 3rd Floor, Mancon Building, McArthur Highway, Calvario, Meycauayan City
  • San Fernando, Pampanga – 3rd Floor, Cleofers Building, Sta. Lucia, Gen. Hizon Extension, San Fernando

Benefits for KBKai Members

As part of this partnership, all incoming Senior High School students who are members of KBKai are entitled to EJIST’s educational programs and support services, including:

  • Access to technical and vocational tracks alongside academic strands
  • Special enrollment assistance and orientation programs
  • Opportunities to participate in community and skill-building projects
  • Student financial aid

This collaboration underscores KBKai’s mission to empower youth through education, providing members with pathways to success in both academics and future careers.


Enrollment Requirements for Senior High School

KBK members who wants enroll at EJIST should prepare the following:

  1. Grade 10 Report Card or Certificate of Completion
  2. PSA Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
  3. Completed EJIST Enrollment Form
  4. 2×2 ID Photos
  5. Valid ID of Parents/Guardian
  6. Additional documents may be required depending on the chosen track or strand

Why EJIST?

Partnering with EJIST means our KBKai youth can benefit from:

  • A safe, supportive, and modern learning environment
  • Diverse academic and technical programs to suit each student’s interests and talents
  • Community-focused initiatives that align with KBKai’s mission of youth empowerment

With EJIST, KBKai members are not just enrolling, they are stepping into a world of opportunities, equipped to succeed in education and beyond.
