Kasangga ng Batang Kankaloo Association Inc. (KBKAI) Partnership with Emmanuel John Institute of Science and Technology (EJIST)


Introduction

Kasangga ng Batang Kankaloo Association Inc. (KBKAI) proudly announce the partnership with Emmanuel John Institute of Science and Technology (EJIST), a leading educational institution in the Philippines dedicated to providing quality, career-oriented education. This partnership ensures that our members have exclusive access to high-quality academic and technical programs, helping young learners achieve their dreams and build a strong foundation for their future.


Who is EJIST

EJIST is committed to nurturing students into competent, responsible, and socially conscious individuals. With modern facilities, skilled faculty, and a holistic approach to education, EJIST offers programs that combine academic excellence with technical skills, preparing students for college, careers, and life.



Campuses

EJIST has several campuses to serve students across Luzon, ensuring accessibility for all members:

  • Caloocan, Metro Manila – 237 M. H. Del Pilar Street, Grace Park, Caloocan City
  • Valenzuela, Metro Manila – 254 Marvic Building, McArthur Highway, Karuhatan, Valenzuela City
  • Pililla, Rizal – Sitio Bulacan 1, Barangay Bagumbayan, Pililla, Rizal
  • Meycauayan, Bulacan – 3rd Floor, Mancon Building, McArthur Highway, Calvario, Meycauayan City
  • San Fernando, Pampanga – 3rd Floor, Cleofers Building, Sta. Lucia, Gen. Hizon Extension, San Fernando

Benefits for KBKAI Members

As part of this partnership, all incoming Senior High School students who are members of KBKAI are entitled to EJIST’s educational programs and support services, including:

  • Access to technical and vocational tracks alongside academic strands
  • Special enrollment assistance and orientation programs
  • Opportunities to participate in community and skill-building projects
  • Student financial aid

This collaboration underscores KBKAI’s mission to empower youth through education, providing members with pathways to success in both academics and future careers.


Enrollment Requirements for Senior High School

KBK members who wants enroll at EJIST should prepare the following:

  1. Grade 10 Report Card or Certificate of Completion
  2. PSA Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
  3. Completed EJIST Enrollment Form
  4. 2×2 ID Photos
  5. Valid ID of Parents/Guardian
  6. Additional documents may be required depending on the chosen track or strand

Why EJIST?

Partnering with EJIST means our KBKAI youth can benefit from:

  • A safe, supportive, and modern learning environment
  • Diverse academic and technical programs to suit each student’s interests and talents
  • Community-focused initiatives that align with KBKAI’s mission of youth empowerment

With EJIST, KBKAI members are not just enrolling, they are stepping into a world of opportunities, equipped to succeed in education and beyond.

Kasangga ng Batang Kankaloo Association Inc. (KBKAI) Partnership with JBEST School of Technology and Practical Skills, Inc.


Introduction

Kasangga ng Batang Kankaloo Association Inc. (KBKAI) is proud to announce its partnership with JBEST School of Technology and Practical Skills, Inc., a leading institution dedicated in providing quality education and skills training for Filipino youth. This collaboration aims to open more opportunities for senior high school students and our members to gain practical knowledge and career-ready skills.


Who is JBEST?

JBEST School of Technology and Practical Skills, Inc. is a reputable educational institution that focuses on equipping students with technical, technological, and practical skills necessary for the 21st-century workforce. Their programs are designed to combine academic learning with hands-on training, ensuring students are ready for both higher education and employment.


What JBEST Can Provide for Senior High School Students

Through this partnership, incoming senior highschool students has the opportunity to access:

  • Specialized technical-vocational courses tailored to market demands
  • Hands-on training in technology, entrepreneurship, and practical skills
  • Competency-based programs that enhance employability and career readiness
  • Opportunities for internships and real-world application of learned skills

Campuses

JBEST has multiple campuses strategically located for accessibility, including:

  • Lagro Campus 4th Floor Lester Building, Km. 22 Quirino Highway, Brgy. Pasong Putik, Lagro, Novaliches, Quezon City.
  • Cubao Campus 4UFG1‑4, 2nd Floor Opulent Building, EDSA corner Gen. Roxas Avenue, Brgy. Socorro, Cubao, Quezon City.

Benefits for KBKAI Members

Members of KBKAI will enjoy advantages, including:

  • Priority enrollment and reserved slots for courses
  • Free of tuition and fees, with no payment required at the time of submission
  • Access to skill-building workshops and seminars
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals and mentors

Enrollment Requirements

To enroll, students need to submit:

  1. Grade 10 Report Card (Form 138) – proof of completion of junior high
  2. Grade 10 Diploma – school completion certificate
  3. PSA/NSO Birth Certificate – for identification and age verification

Why KBKAI Chose JBEST as a Partner?

We chose JBEST School of Technology and Practical Skills, Inc. because of their strong commitment to quality education, practical training, and holistic development of students. Their focus on skills that meet the demands of the modern workforce aligns perfectly with KBKAI’s mission to empower our young ones to achieve academic and professional excellence.


Through this partnership, KBKAI members now have a reliable pathway to enhance their skills, secure meaningful opportunities, and prepare for a brighter future.
